    What to do if you are sick?

    • Confirm your diagnosis with a test from your nearest hospital.
    • Stay at home.
    • Stay in touch with your doctor.
    • Separate yourself from others.
    • Monitor your symptoms.
    • Call ahead before visting your doctor or hospital.
    • Wear a mask covering nose and mouth when around pets or people even at home.
    • Avoid sharing household items like kitchen utensils, etc.

    What to do if your loved one is sick?

    • Confirm their diagnosis with a test from your nearest hospital.
    • Stay at home.
    • Avoid sharing spaces with the sick one.
    • Keep their doctor's number handy.
    • Monitor their symptoms.
    • Give them plenty of fluids like soup,etc.
    • Call ahead before visting the doctor or hospital.
    • Wear a mask covering nose and mouth when attending to the sick.
    • Wear gloves when attending to th sick, discard after each use and wash your hands thoroughly for 20 seconds.
    • Avoid sharing household items like kitchen utensils, etc.
    • Clean and disinfect surfaces and items which are frequently touched, every day. This includes tables, doorknobs, light switches, handles, desks, toilets, faucets, sinks, and electronics.

    How to run essential errands?

    • Stay at home if you are sick.

    • Getting groceries:

      1. Order grocereis online
      2. If going out for shopping, stay 6 feet away from other people and wear a mask.
      3. Disinfect the shopping cart, use disinfecting wipes if available.
      4. If you handle money, a card, or use a keypad, use hand sanitizer right after paying.
      5. After coming home wash your hands and disinfect the groceries using a household disinfectant or water, depending on the type of item.

    • Banking:

      1. Opt for online banking servies.
      2. If going out stay 6 feet apart from ther people and wear a mask. Make sure that bank employees and other people inside the bank are also wearing a mask.
      3. Use hand sanitizer containing at least 60% alcohol after any deposit, withdrawal, exchange, or use of an ATM.
      4. After coming home wash your hands thoroughly for 20 secs.

    Funeral Guidance:

    • The funeral rituals should have minimal possible number of immediate family and relatives. All in attendance should observe social distancing of at least 6 feet, wear face masks and frequent hand washes.
    • Clothes worn by the person preparing the body should be immediately removed after procedure, washed with warm water at 60−90°C and laundry detergent.
    • Anyone handling the belongings of deceased should wear gloves. The belongings should be disinfected with 70% ethanol. The household should be disinfected using 0.5% chlorine or 0.1% bleach solution..
    • The clothes of the deceased or fabrics used like linen, towels etc. should be washed in a machine using laundry detergent and warm water at 60−90°C
